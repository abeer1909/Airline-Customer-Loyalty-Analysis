
# Airline Customer Loyalty Program Analysis

## Overview

Analysis for fictitious customer loyalty based in Canada. It provides. The goal is to evaluate customer engagement, flight activity, reward point distribution, and retention performance through data-driven insights.
## About the dataset

This comprehensive dataset encompasses information on all customers, including detailed profiles of each individual.

## Insights Drawn
![Dashboard](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1EayninwdZjElazcdTtvZi082fdA8o88o/view?usp=share_link)
- Even though only 6% of the customers opted for customer loyalty program through standard promotion but customers form 2018 promotion result in 40% of top 5 customers.
- Majority of cutomers are young, the are Bachelors students, College students or are in high school or below.
- Single people travel the most.
- Ontario is the most successful province for the program.
- Female spend higher than males in terms of flights.
- Major issue is that redemption rate is very less, it is under 2%, to increase the customers in loyalty program there is a need to increase that.
